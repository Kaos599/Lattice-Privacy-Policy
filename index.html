Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

Lattice ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to your data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Lattice is designed to prioritize your privacy. We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services.

A. User-Provided Content (Diagrams)

When you use Lattice to evaluate a diagram on Excalidraw or other supported platforms:

  • What we do: We temporarily capture an image of your active diagram and the text/code context you verify in the side panel.
  • How it is used: This data is sent directly to the Google Gemini API (via your own API key) for analysis.
  • Retention: We do not store your diagrams, code, or context on our servers. The data is processed ephemerally by the AI model to generate the feedback report.

B. API Keys

  • What we do: We require you to provide your own Google Gemini API Key.
  • How it is used: This key is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage.local API. It is used exclusively to authenticate requests to Google's generative AI services.
  • Retention: We never transmit your API key to any server other than Google's API endpoints. We do not have access to your key.

C. Extension Usage Data

  • We do not run any third-party analytics trackers (like Google Analytics or Mixpanel) inside the extension.
  • We do not track your browsing history. The extension only activates on specific domains (e.g., Excalidraw) when you explicitly open the side panel or click the action button.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service to provide AI analysis:

  • Google Gemini API: We send the diagram image and context to Google's servers for processing. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Generative AI Terms of Service.

3. Data Storage

  • Local History: Lattice saves a history of your past evaluations (reports and scores) locally on your browser. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it.
  • Settings: Your preferences (e.g., theme, API key) are stored locally using the Chrome Storage API.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not store your data on our servers, there is no need to request account deletion from us. You have full control over your data to:

  • Delete History: You can clear your evaluation history at any time from the "History" tab in the extension.
  • Remove API Key: You can delete your API key from the "Settings" tab.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data.
