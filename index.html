Privacy Policy

System Architecture Evaluator

Lattice ("we", "our", or "us") is designed to prioritize your privacy. We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services.

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

Data Collection & Usage

A. User-Provided Content

When you use Lattice to evaluate a diagram on Excalidraw:

  • Ephemeral Processing: We temporarily capture an image of your active diagram and the text/code context you verify.
  • Direct API Transmission: This data is sent directly to the Google Gemini API (using your personal API key).
  • Zero Retention: We do not store your diagrams, code, or context on our servers. The data exists only in transit to Google's API.

B. API Keys

  • Local Storage only: Your Google Gemini API Key is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage.local API.
  • No Access: We never transmit your API key to any server other than Google's API endpoints.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service to provide AI analysis:

  • Google Gemini API: We send the diagram image and context to Google's servers for processing. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Generative AI Terms of Service.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data locally:

  • Delete History: Clear your evaluation history at any time from the "History" tab in the extension.
  • Remove API Key: Delete your API key instantly from the "Settings" tab.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the extension permanently removes all locally stored data.
